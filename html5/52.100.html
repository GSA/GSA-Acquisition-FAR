52.100 Scope of subpart.

This subpart-

  1. (a) Gives instructions for using part  52, including the explanation and use of provision and clause numbers, prescriptions, prefaces, and the matrix;

  2. (b) Prescribes procedures for incorporating, identifying, and modifying provisions and clauses in solicitations and contracts, and for using alternates; and

  3. (c) Describes the derivation of FAR provisions and clauses.
