28.105-2 Patent infringement bonds.

  1. (a) Contracts providing for patent indemnity may require these bonds only if-

    1. (1) A performance bond is not furnished; and

    2. (2) The financial responsibility of the contractor is unknown or doubtful.

  2. (b) The contracting officer shall determine the penal sum.
