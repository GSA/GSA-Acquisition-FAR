23.401 Definitions.

As used in this subpart-

  1. (a) "EPA-designated item" means a product that is or can be made with recovered material-

    1. (1) That is listed by EPA in a procurement guideline (40 CFR part 247); and

    2. (2) For which EPA has provided purchasing recommendations in a related Recovered Materials Advisory Notice (RMAN) (available at https://www.epa.gov/smm/comprehensive-procurement-guideline-cpg-program).

  2. (b) "USDA-designated item" means a generic grouping of products that are or can be made with biobased materials-

    1. (1) That is listed by USDA in a procurement guideline (7 CFR part 3201, subpart B); and

    2. (2) For which USDA has provided purchasing recommendations.
