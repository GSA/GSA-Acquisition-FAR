15.205 Issuing solicitations.

  1. (a) The contracting officer shall issue solicitations to potential sources in accordance with the policies and procedures in 5.102, 19.202-4, and part  6.

  2. (b) A master solicitation, as described in 14.203-3, may also be used for negotiated acquisitions.
