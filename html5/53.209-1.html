53.209-1 Responsible prospective contractors.

The following forms are prescribed for use in conducting preaward surveys of prospective contractors, as specified in 9.106-1 , 9.106-2 , and 9.106-4 . These forms are authorized for local reproduction and can be found at the GSA Forms Library at https://www.gsa.gov/forms .

  1. (a) SF 1403 (Rev. 01/2014), Preaward Survey of Prospective Contractor (General).

  2. (b) SF 1404 (Rev. 01/2014), Preaward Survey of Prospective Contractor-Technical.

  3. (c) SF 1405 (Rev. 01/2014), Preaward Survey of Prospective Contractor-Production.

  4. (d) SF 1406 (Rev. 01/2014), Preaward Survey of Prospective Contractor-Quality Assurance.

  5. (e) SF 1407 (Rev. 01/2014), Preaward Survey of Prospective Contractor-Financial Capability.

  6. (f) SF 1408 (Rev. 01/2014), Preaward Survey of Prospective Contractor-Accounting System.

Parent topic: 53.209 Contractor qualifications.
