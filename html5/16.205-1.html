16.205-1 Description.

A fixed-price contract with prospective price redetermination provides for-

  1. (a) A firm fixed price for an initial period of contract deliveries or performance; and

  2. (b) Prospective redetermination, at a stated time or times during performance, of the price for subsequent periods of performance.
