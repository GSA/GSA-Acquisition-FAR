25.000 Scope of part.

  1. (a) This part provides policies and procedures for-

    1. (1) Acquisition of foreign supplies, services, and construction materials; and

    2. (2) Contracts performed outside the United States.

  2. (b) It implements 41 U.S.C. chapter 83, Buy American; trade agreements; and other laws and regulations.
