3.1004 Contract clauses.

  1. (a) Insert the clause at FAR 52.203-13, Contractor Code of Business Ethics and Conduct, in solicitations and contracts if the value of the contract is expected to exceed $6 million and the performance period is 120 days or more.

  2. (b)

    1. (1) Unless the contract is for the acquisition of a commercial product or commercial service or will be performed entirely outside the United States, insert the clause at 52.203-14, Display of Hotline Poster(s), if–

      1. (i) The contract exceeds $6 million or a lesser amount established by the agency; and

      2. (ii)

        1. (A) The agency has a fraud hotline poster; or

        2. (B) The contract is funded with disaster assistance funds.

    2. (2) In paragraph (b)(3) of the clause, the contracting officer shall–

      1. (i) Identify the applicable posters; and

      2. (ii) Insert the website link(s) or other contact information for obtaining the agency and/or Department of Homeland Security poster.

    3. (3) In paragraph (d) of the clause, if the agency has established policies and procedures for display of the OIG fraud hotline poster at a lesser amount, the contracting officer shall replace " $6 million " with the lesser amount that the agency has established.
