15.200 Scope of subpart.

This subpart prescribes policies and procedures for-

  1. (a) Exchanging information with industry prior to receipt of proposals;

  2. (b) Preparing and issuing requests for proposals (RFPs) and requests for information (RFIs); and

  3. (c) Receiving proposals and information.

Parent topic: Subpart 15.2 - Solicitation and Receipt of Proposals and Information
