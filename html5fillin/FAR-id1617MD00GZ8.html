1.603-2 Selection.

In selecting contracting officers, the appointing official shall consider the complexity and dollar value of the acquisitions to be assigned and the candidateâ€™s experience, training, education, business acumen, judgment, character, and reputation. Examples of selection criteria include-

  1. (a) Experience in Government contracting and administration, commercial purchasing, or related fields;

  2. (b) Education or special training in business administration, law, accounting, engineering, or related fields;

  3. (c) Knowledge of acquisition policies and procedures, including this and other applicable regulations;

  4. (d) Specialized knowledge in the particular assigned field of contracting; and

  5. (e) Satisfactory completion of acquisition training courses.
