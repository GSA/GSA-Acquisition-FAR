36.213-4 Notice of award.

When a notice of award is issued, it shall be done in writing or electronically, shall contain information required by 14.408 , and shall-

  1. (a) Identify the invitation for bids;

  2. (b) Identify the contractorâ€™s bid;

  3. (c) State the award price;

  4. (d) Advise the contractor that any required payment and performance bonds must be promptly executed and returned to the contracting officer;

  5. (e) Specify the date of commencement of work, or advise that a notice to proceed will be issued.
