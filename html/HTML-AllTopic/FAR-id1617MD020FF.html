22.102-1 Policy.

Agencies shall cooperate, and encourage contractors to cooperate with Federal and State agencies responsible for enforcing labor requirements such as-

  1. (a) Safety;

  2. (b) Health and sanitation;

  3. (c) Maximum hours and minimum wages;

  4. (d) Equal employment opportunity;

  5. (e) Child and convict labor;

  6. (f) Age discrimination;

  7. (g) Disabled and Vietnam veteran employment;

  8. (h) Employment of workers with disabilities; and

  9. (i) Eligibility for employment under United States immigration laws.

Parent topic: 22.102 Federal and State labor requirements.
