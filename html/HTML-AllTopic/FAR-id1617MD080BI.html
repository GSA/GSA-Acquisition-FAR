25.501 General.

The contracting officer-

  1. (a) Must apply the evaluation procedures of this subpart to each line item of an offer unless either the offer or the solicitation specifies evaluation on a group basis (see 25.503);

  2. (b) May rely on the offerorâ€™s certification of end product origin when evaluating a foreign offer;

  3. (c) Must identify and reject offers of end products that are prohibited in accordance with subpart  25.7; and
