33.202 Disputes.

41 U.S.C. chapter 71 , Disputes, establishes procedures and requirements for asserting and resolving claims subject to the Disputes statute. In addition, the Disputes statute provides for-

  1. (a) The payment of interest on contractor claims;

  2. (b) Certification of contractor claims; and

  3. (c) A civil penalty for contractor claims that are fraudulent or based on a misrepresentation of fact.
