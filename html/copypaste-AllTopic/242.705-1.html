242.705-1 Contracting officer determination procedure.

(a) Applicability and responsibility.

(1) The corporate administrative contracting officer and individual administrative contracting officers shall jointly decide how to conduct negotiations. Follow the procedures at PGI PGI 242.705-1 Contracting officer determination procedure. (a)(1) when negotiations are conducted on a coordinated basis.
