571.102 Purpose.

GSA has developed this pilot program to be implemented outside the normal Federal Acquisition Regulations requirements to engage traditional and non-traditional Government contractors, including start-up companies. This program is intended to promote competition with a streamlined approach to address specific needs for innovative commercial items.       (c)  Simplified contract administration procedures and requirements; and

      (d)  Preference for the vendor retaining core intellectual property, as appropriate.
