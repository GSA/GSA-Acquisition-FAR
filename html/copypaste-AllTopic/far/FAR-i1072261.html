46.304 Fixed-price service contracts.

The contracting officer shall insert the clause at 52.246-4 , Inspection of Services-Fixed-Price, in solicitations and contracts for services, or supplies that involve the furnishing of services, when a fixed-price contract is contemplated and the contract amount is expected to exceed the simplified acquisition threshold.
