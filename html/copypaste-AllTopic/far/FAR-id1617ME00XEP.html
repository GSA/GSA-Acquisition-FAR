53.229 Taxes (SF’s 1094, 1094-A).

SF 1094(Rev.12/96), U.S. Tax Exemption Form, and SF 1094A(Rev.12/96), Tax Exemption Forms Accountability Record. SF’s 1094 and 1094A are prescribed for use in establishing exemption from State or local taxes, as specified in 29.302 (b).
