32.101 Authority.

The basic authority for the contract financing described in this part is contained in 41 U.S.C. chapter 45 , Contract Financing, 10 U.S.C.2307 , and TitleIII of the Defense Production Act of l950 ( 50 U.S.C. App.2091 ).
