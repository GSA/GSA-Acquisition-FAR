Subpart 34.2 - Earned Value Management System

Parent topic: Part 34 - Major System Acquisition

34.201 Policy.

(a) In accordance with OMB Circular A-11, agencies must require an Earned Value Management System (EVMS) in major acquisitions for development. Agencies may consider requiring an EVMS for other acquisitions, as appropriate.

(b) For contracts with EVMS requirements, agencies must require contractor submission of monthly EVMS reports.

(c) EVMS requirements will be applied to subcontractors using the same rules as applied to the prime contractor.

(d) When required in a solicitation, agencies will determine the adequacy of a contractor-proposed EVMS plan prior to contract award.

34.202 Integrated Baseline Reviews.

When an EVMS is required, the agency will conduct an Integrated Baseline Review.

34.203 Contract clause.

Insert a clause that is substantially the same as the clause at FAR 52.234-4, Earned Value Management System, in solicitations and contracts that require a contractor to use an EVMS.
