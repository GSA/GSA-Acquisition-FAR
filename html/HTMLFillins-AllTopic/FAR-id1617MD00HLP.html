3.904 Procedures for filing complaints.

  1. (a) Any employee of a contractor who believes that he or she has been discharged, demoted, or otherwise discriminated against contrary to the policy in 3.903 may file a complaint with the Inspector General of the agency that awarded the contract.

  2. (b) The complaint shall be signed and shall contain-

    1. (1) The name of the contractor;

    2. (2) The contract number, if known; if not, a description reasonably sufficient to identify the contract(s) involved;

    3. (3) The substantial violation of law giving rise to the disclosure;

    4. (4) The nature of the disclosure giving rise to the discriminatory act; and

    5. (5) The specific nature and date of the reprisal.
