552.238-86 Delivery Schedule.

As prescribed in 538.273(d)(10) insert the following clause:

Delivery Schedule (May 2019)

  1. (a)  Time of delivery. The Contractor shall deliver to destination within the number of calendar days after receipt of order (ARO) in the case of F.O.B. Destination prices; or to place of shipment in transit in the case of F.O.B. Origin prices, as set forth below. Offerors shall insert in the ''Time of Delivery (days ARO)'' column in the schedule of Items a definite number of calendar days within which delivery will be made. In no case shall the offered delivery time exceed the Contractor's normal business practice. The Government requires the Contractor's normal delivery time, as long as it is less than the ''stated'' delivery time(s) shown below.   3. (c)  Overnight and 2-Day delivery times. Ordering activities may require overnight or 2-day delivery.
