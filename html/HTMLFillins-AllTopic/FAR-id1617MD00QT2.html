9.303 Use.

Testing and approval may be appropriate when-

  1. (a) The contractor has not previously furnished the product to the Government;

  2. (b) The contractor previously furnished the product to the Government, but-

    1. (1) There have been subsequent changes in processes or specifications;

    2. (2) Production has been discontinued for an extended period of time; or

    3. (3) The product acquired under a previous contract developed a problem during its life;

  3. (c) The product is described by a performance specification; or

  4. (d) It is essential to have an approved first article to serve as a manufacturing standard.
