52.252-5 Authorized Deviations in Provisions.

Authorized Deviations in Provisions (Apr 1984)

  1. (a)The use in this solicitation of any Federal Acquisition Regulation (48 CFR Chapter 1) provision with an authorized deviation is indicated by the addition of “(DEVIATION)” after the date of the provision.

  2. (b)The use in this solicitation of any (48 CFR Chapter) provision with an authorized deviation is indicated by the addition of “(DEVIATION)” after the name of the regulation.

(End of clause)
