13.006 Inapplicable provisions and clauses.

While certain statutes still apply, pursuant to Public Law103-355, the following provisions and clauses are inapplicable to contracts and subcontracts at or below the simplified acquisition threshold:

  1. (a) 52.203-5, Covenant Against Contingent Fees.

  2. (b) 52.203-6, Restrictions on Subcontractor Sales to the Government.

  3. (c) 52.203-7, Anti-Kickback Procedures.

  4. (d) 52.215-2, Audits and Records-Negotiation, except as used with its Alternate I, when using funds appropriated or otherwise made available by the American Recovery and Reinvestment Act of 2009 (Pub. L. 111-5).

  5. (e) 52.222-4, Contract Work Hours and Safety Standards-Overtime Compensation.

  6. (f) 52.223-6, Drug-Free Workplace, except for individuals.

  7. (g) 52.223-9, Estimate of Percentage of Recovered Material Content for EPA-Designated Items.
