19.304 Small disadvantaged business status.

  1. (a) The contracting officer may accept an offerorâ€™s representation that it is a small disadvantaged business concern (SDB) concern.

  2. (b) The provision at 52.219-1, Small Business Program Representations, or 52.212-3(c)(4), Offeror Representations and Certifications-Commercial Items, is used to collect SDB data.

  3. (c) A representation of SDB status on a Federal prime contract will be deemed a misrepresentation of SDB status if the firm does not meet the requirements of 13 CFR 124.1001(b).

  4. (d) Any person or entity that misrepresents a firm's status as an SDB concern in order to obtain a contracting opportunity in accordance with section 8(d) of the Small Business Act, ( 15 U.S.C. 637(d)) will be subject to the penalties imposed by section 16(d) of the Small Business Act, ( 15 U.S.C. 645(d)), as well as any other penalty authorized by law.
