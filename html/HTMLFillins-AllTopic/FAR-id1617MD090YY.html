25.702-3 Remedies.

Upon the determination of a false certification under subsection 25.702-2 -

  1. (a) The contracting officer may terminate the contract;

  2. (b) The suspending official may suspend the contractor in accordance with the procedures in subpart  9.4; and

  3. (c) The debarring official may debar the contractor for a period not to exceed 3 years in accordance with the procedures in subpart  9.4.
