22.1205 Notification to contractors and service employees.

  1. (a) The contracting officer shall direct that the predecessor contractor provides written notice to service employees of their possible right to an offer of employment with the successor contractor. The written notice shall be-

    1. (1) Posted in a conspicuous place at the worksite; or

    2. (2) Delivered to the service employees individually. If such delivery is via e-mail, the notification must result in an electronic delivery receipt or some other reliable confirmation that the intended recipient received the notice.

  2. (b) Contracting officers may advise contractors to provide the notice in Appendix B to 29 CFR chapter 9.
