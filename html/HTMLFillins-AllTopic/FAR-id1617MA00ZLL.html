3.1000 Scope of subpart.

This subpart-

  1. (a) Implements 41 U.S.C. 3509, Notification of Violations of Federal Criminal Law or Overpayments; and

  2. (b) Prescribes policies and procedures for the establishment of contractor codes of business ethics and conduct, and display of agency Office of Inspector General (OIG) fraud hotline posters.
