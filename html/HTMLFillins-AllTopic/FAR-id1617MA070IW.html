23.800 Scope of subpart.

This subpart-

  1. (a) Sets forth policies and procedures for the acquisition of items that-

    1. (1) Contain, use, or are manufactured with ozone-depleting substances; or

    2. (2) Contain or use high global warming potential hydrofluorocarbons; and

  2. (b) Addresses public disclosure of greenhouse gas emissions and reduction goals.
