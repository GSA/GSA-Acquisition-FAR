52.226-4 Notice of Disaster or Emergency Area Set-Aside.

As prescribed in 26.206(b), insert the following clause:

Notice of Disaster or Emergency Area set-Aside (Nov 2007)

  1. (a) Set-aside area. Offers are solicited only from businesses residing or primarily doing business in Offers received from other businesses shall not be considered.

  2. (b) This set-aside is in addition to any small business set-aside contained in this contract.

(End of clause)
