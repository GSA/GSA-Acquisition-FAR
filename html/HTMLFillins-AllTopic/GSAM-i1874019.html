552.238-77 Submission and Distribution of Authorized Federal Supply Schedule (FSS) Price Lists.

As prescribed in 538.273(d)(1), insert the following clause:

Submission and Distribution of Authorized Federal Supply Schedule (FSS) Price Lists (Mar 2020)

  1. (a) The Contractor shall submit its Authorized Federal Supply Schedule Price List on a common-use electronic medium as prescribed by GSA.  Some structured data entry in a prescribed format may be required.

  2. (b) Eligible ordering activities will utilize GSA’s online shopping and ordering system to review a Contractors’ price lists.

(End of clause)
