Time-and-Materials/Labor-Hour Proposal Requirements-Non-Commercial Item Acquisition Without Adequate Price Competition (Feb 2020)

  1. (a) The Government contemplates award of a Time-and-Materials or Labor-Hour type of contract resulting from this solicitation.

  2. (b) The offeror must specify separate fixed hourly rates in its offer that include wages, overhead, general and administrative expenses, and profit for each category of labor to be performed by—

    1. (1) The offeror;

    2. (2) Each subcontractor; and

    3. (3) Each division, subsidiary, or affiliate of the offeror under a common control.

  3. (c)Unless exempt under paragraph (d) of this provision, the fixed hourly rates for services transferred between divisions, subsidiaries, or affiliates of the offeror under a common control—

    1. (1)Shall not include profit for the transferring organization; but

    2. (2)May include profit for the prime Contractor.

  4. (d)Orders may be issued by methods other than those enumerated in this clause only if authorized in the contract.

(End of provision)
