52.247-19 Stopping in Transit for Partial Unloading.

Stopping in Transit for Partial Unloading (Apr 1984)

When multiple shipments are tendered at one time to the Contractor for movement from one origin to two or more consignees along the route between the origin and the last destination, the rate charged shall be the rate applicable to the aggregate weight, plus a charge of $ for each shipment unloaded at an intermediate point en route to the last destination.

(End of clause)
