22.1701 Applicability.

  1. (a) This subpart applies to all acquisitions.

  2. (b) The requirement at 22.1703(c) for a certification and compliance plan applies only to any portion of a contract or subcontract that-

    1. (1) Is for supplies, other than commercially available off-the-shelf (COTS) items, to be acquired outside the United States, or services to be performed outside the United States; and

    2. (2) Has an estimated value that exceeds $500,000.
