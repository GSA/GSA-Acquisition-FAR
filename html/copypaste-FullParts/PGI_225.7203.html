PGI 225.7203 Contracting officer distribution of reports.

Before contract award, forward a copy of any reports that are submitted with offers in accordance with the provision at 252.225-7003 Report of Intended Performance Outside the United States and Canada—Submission with Offer., Report of Intended Performance Outside the United States and Canada—Submission with Offer, to the Deputy Director of Defense Procurement and Acquisition Policy (Contract Policy and International Contracting), OUSD(AT&L) DPAP/CPIC, Washington, DC 20301-3060. This is necessary to satisfy the requirement of 10 U.S.C. 2410g that notifications (or copies) of contract performance outside the United States and Canada be maintained in compiled form for 5 years after the date of submission.
